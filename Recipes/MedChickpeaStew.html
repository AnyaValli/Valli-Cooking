Mediterranean Chickpea Stew

Cook time: 30 minutes

Ingredients

  • 300g chickpeas, soaked overnight
  • 2 red onions, diced
  • 2 gloves garlic, crushed
  • 1 tsp dried oregano
  • 1 tsp cayenne pepper
  • 1/4 teaspoon dried cloves
  • 1 (400g) can chopped tomatoes
  • 250ml vegetable stock
  • 1 tablespoon balsamic vinegar
  • 1 bag fresh spinach
  • 1 block crumbled feta
  • 1 pack tenderstem broccoli
  • 1 chilli, diced
  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Method

  1. Heat olive oil over a medium heat in a large skillet or sauce pot. Add the onions and sauté for 5 minutes to soften. Add the garlic, oregano, cayenne and cloves and cook another minute.

  2. Stir in the tomatoes, stock, salt, pepper and vinegar. Bring to a boil and then reduce the heat down to a simmer. Let cook 7-10 minutes to thicken the sauce - stir every so often.

  3. Meanwhile, drizzle the broccoli with olive oil in a large bowl, and toss with salt, pepper and garlic powder. Cook in the air fryer at 190C for 8 minutes, shaking halfway through

  4. Going back to the main stew, stir in the chickpeas, spinach and half the feta. Cover and cook another 2-3 minutes. Then add the roasted broccoli and other half of the feta, stir until mixed in, then serve.
